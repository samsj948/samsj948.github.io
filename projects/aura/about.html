About the Process

Starting with a simple survey asking questions about who people are, what they think, and how they feel the auras began to form. After researching about the different colors and sections that go into aura mapping, I assigned a color to each answer choice. To create the auras I went through each question to see what answer the person chose and put the corresponding colors together based on a set map I had drawn out. Since every person had different answer combinations, it created an array of multicolored artworks. Within each one I chose the exact colors based on what it was around as well, so even if two people had exactly the same answers the colors and location would still be unique. The boxes on the auras are draggable so if one would like to save the image they can get the full picture.
